How to order

To order some clothes just follow these steps

Browse some clothes on our facebook posts

Select the clothes you desire on our posts or albums

Send a personal message

Using facebook messenger share the posts of the clothes selected and type your desired message about your order. Include the important details on where and when to meetup for you to get your orders.

Meetup

Since you gave some details on where to pickup, our delivery person shall bring your order to your meetup place. Then there you will do your payment
